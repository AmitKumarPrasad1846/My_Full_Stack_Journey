extra notes and learning

  1. what if i say i want to color 2 para in the div same color and 1 ,3 para red color then

    div p:nth-child(2) selects the second paragraph inside each div, applying a blue background with white text color.

  2. div p:first-child targets the first paragraph inside each div and applies a yellow background with red text color.
  3. div p:not(:first-child) selects all other paragraphs inside the div and applies a blue background with white text color.
