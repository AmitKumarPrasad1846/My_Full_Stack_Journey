Margin Collapse :

In CSS, margin collapse happens when two vertical margins meet, and instead of adding up, they collapse into one — and that one will be the larger of the two, not the sum.

Let's understand this margin collapse concept with an example.

⚔️ Imagine two warriors: if one has a 30px sword and the other a 20px, they don’t combine to swing a 50px blade. The bigger sword wins — that’s what shows up in the layout.
